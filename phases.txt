## My recommendation

The next feature I'd build is **Reviews and Ratings**.

Why?

* It's expected in a Yellow Pages app.
* It ties together both user roles:

  * Normal users contribute reviews.
  * Business users receive feedback.
* It gives your service cards a much richer look with star ratings and review counts.

Adding reviews will make Lemon Page feel much closer to a real directory platform like Yelp or Google Business Profiles.
